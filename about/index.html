The Engineer's Paradox

Hey, I'm Kim Edrian Binasoy.

Cloud Engineer. AI Enthusiast. Builder of Relearn.ing.

I spent 4 years architecting automation for banks and transportation networks. I built systems that scaled, deployed Gen-AI bots to streamline knowledge transfer, and optimized workflows for hundreds of employees.

But I couldn't automate my own habits.

By day, I was a disciplined engineer. By night, my personal operating system was a mess of half-started projects and "productivity tools" that only added friction. I became a Tool Collector, hoarding AI agents like infinity stones, hoping they would magically fix my output.

They didn't. I had the best tech stack in the world, but I lacked the system.

Relearn.ing is my open-source attempt to fix that. It is not a blog; it is a documentation of debugging my own habits. It is the application of enterprise-grade engineering discipline—observability, refactoring, and automation—to the messy reality of being human.

The Toolkit

I still use the tools I mastered in the industry—Azure, AWS, Python, TypeScript—but now I use them to build workflows that actually ship.
